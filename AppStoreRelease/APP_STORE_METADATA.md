## Description
FYTRR helps you find better meal options around you and turn daily nutrition into a cleaner routine. Set up your profile, view nearby fuel picks, check your daily targets, and use the map to find restaurants that match your goals.

Built for active people who want less noise and better decisions, FYTRR keeps the experience focused on the essentials: profile setup, daily fuel guidance, nearby restaurant discovery, and a cleaner map-first meal search.

Key features:
- Personalized daily fuel targets
- Nearby meal and restaurant discovery
- Clean map view with readable restaurant cards
- Profile-based recommendations
- Fuel streaks and daily check-ins
- Optional meal reminders
- Apple sign-in and email account support

FYTRR is designed to support everyday nutrition decisions. It is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice.
