# FYTRR First Marketing Video

## Concept

Title: Find Your Fuel
Length: 30 seconds
Primary format: 9:16 vertical
Secondary cuts: 15-second Reels/TikTok, 6-second bumper, App Store preview test

## Core Message

FYTRR helps people choose healthier nearby restaurant meals in seconds, using restaurant data, fuel quality, Apple Health context, and rewards.

## Voiceover

1. You trained. You're hungry. Now the hard part: finding food that actually fits your goals.
2. FYTRR ranks healthier nearby restaurant meals based on fuel quality, protein, calories, and your day.
3. Check the best meal, open the menu, order, or get directions.
4. Earn FYTRR Credits for better choices and streaks.
5. And every 10 meals helps feed a child.
6. FYTRR. Find Your Fuel.

## Shot List

1. Post-workout hunger moment, quick cut, dark gym or car interior.
2. FYTRR app icon and wordmark reveal.
3. Map with nearby restaurants and ranked fuel scores.
4. Best Nearby Fuel card with a high score and protein-forward meal.
5. Credits and streak moment.
6. Mission close: every 10 meals helps feed a child.

## Visual Direction

Use black, FYTRR blue, white, and small green accent moments. Keep typography bold, athletic, and clean. Avoid generic stock-food clutter. The app UI, map, and score should be the hero.

## Suggested Music

Modern athletic beat, 95-115 BPM, clean bass, confident but not aggressive.
